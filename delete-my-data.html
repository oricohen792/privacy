How to Delete Your Data

If you would like your data to be deleted from our servers, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to ori@efrontier.io with the subject line: “Delete My Facebook App Data”.
  2. Include your Facebook User ID or the email address associated with your Facebook account.
  3. We will process your deletion request within 7 business days.

Note: This request applies only to data collected through this application, not your general Facebook or Instagram account data.
